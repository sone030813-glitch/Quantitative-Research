## Bayes Theorem and Examples
# 1.5.1, Discrete case

$$
\Pr(X = x_i \mid Y = y_j)
= \frac{\Pr(X = x_i, Y = y_j)}{\Pr(Y = y_j)}.
$$

which can be rewritten as

$$
\Pr(X = x_i \mid Y = y_j)
= \frac{\Pr(Y = y_j \mid X = x_i)\,\Pr(X = x_i)}{\Pr(Y = y_j)}.
$$
